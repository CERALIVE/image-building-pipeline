# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community. Be respectful, patient, and constructive. Harassment and discrimination are not tolerated.

- Assume good intent and focus on technical solutions
- Critique ideas, not people
- Be helpful to newcomers
- Use inclusive language

Enforcement: Maintainers may moderate issues/PRs to uphold these standards.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential issue or contact the maintainers.
